HDD DEFRAGMENTATION

Let's talk about Hard Drive Defragmentation.

WHAT IS A HARD DISK DRIVE?

Hard Disk Drive (HDD) is a non volatile computer device that controls the writing, reading and positioning of the hard disk which furnishes data storage. Hard disk drives is used as the main storage device of a computer. It stores Operating Systems, Software Applications, and Personal Files. In the computer, HDD is commonly found in the drive bay and connected to the mobo via ATA, SATA or SCSI cable. It is also connected to the PSU which keep stored data while powered on.  

Defragmentation reorganizes all the files stored in your computer's Hard Disk, this will create free space that is required enough to make improvements on your computer's performance. Fragmentation is usually occurs when you save, change or remove files. Windows 7 and Windows 8 automatically defragment your hard drives for you on a schedule. 

WHAT ARE THE ADVANTAGES OF DEFRAGMENTATION?

1. It removes all the damage files that consume huge space on your hard disk. However, unnecessary destroyed files can affect the performance of your hard disk.

2. It checks all the available errors in your hard disk which allows your to open your applications a way faster.

3. It reorganizes your files without losing any of your crucial documents.

4. Your computer works much faster and run smoothly.

WHEN WILL I DEFRAG MY HARD DRIVE?

1. When you copy large files to your hard disk.

2. When you install lots of applications to your computer.

3. When you delete massive files on your computer.

HOW TO DEFRAG YOUR HARD DISK DRIVE ON WINDOWS?

1. Right Click on Local Drive (C:).
2. Click Properties and a window will appear.
3. On the Window tab, click on Tools.
4. Click the Defragment Now button.
5. Click Analyze Disk wait until finished analyzing.
6. then Click Defragment Disk.
It will take sometime to defrag your Hard Drive.
